About Us

Cambridge University Riviera Racing is a team of innovative students driven together by a common aim – a more sustainable maritime future. To prove the viability of hydrogen as a fuel source for this industry, we are currently building our first zero-emissions boat to compete at the 2024 Monaco Energy Boat Challenge.

Our Concept

Our hydrogen fuel-cell catamaran racing boat, expected to reach speeds above 20 knots and use less power than a kettle, is intended to be a showcase of the cutting edge of hydrogen fuel technology.

Using a bespoke propulsion system and combining the efforts and expertise of some of the brightest minds in Cambridge and the maritime industry, we hope to serve as a showcase of the talent and innovation on offer when we aim high.

Competition Timeline for 2023 - 2024
